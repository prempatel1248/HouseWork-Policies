1. Services overview

HouseWork connects households (people requesting household tasks) with helpers (service providers). Households post tasks with date, time window, tasks, and price. Helpers can view nearby requests and accept those they wish to perform. After a helper accepts a request, the App shares contact details so the parties can coordinate directly.

2. Roles & eligibility

The App supports two roles: Household and Helper. You must be 18 years or older to use the App. You agree to provide accurate information at registration and to update it when necessary.

3. Payments, commission & cancellation fees (v1)

  • Primary payments between household and helper: In this first release, households pay helpers directly (cash, UPI, bank transfer, etc.). The App does not process the main job payment.
  • Platform commission: Helpers agree to pay a commission on earnings for jobs accepted/completed via the App. The current commission rate is 10%. We may change the commission rate; changes will be posted in the App and on our website or policy pages.
  • Cancellation fee: If an accepted request is cancelled by either party, a cancellation fee equal to 10% of the request price may be applied. This rate can be updated by us. For v1 the App records and manages these fees (and will charge via the chosen payment flow when enabled).
